Our Story

From a Bold Idea to a Thriving Agency.

Stackly was born in 2018 when our founders recognized a gap in the market — agencies were either great at strategy but poor at execution, or creative but not data-driven. We set out to build something different: a full-service agency that combined rigorous analytical thinking with world-class creative output.

What started as a two-person operation has grown into a team of 50+ strategists, creatives, engineers, and marketers serving clients across four continents. We've worked with startups ready to scale and established enterprises looking to reinvent their digital presence.

Today, Stackly is recognized as one of the fastest-growing independent agencies, with a reputation for delivering campaigns that don't just look beautiful — they perform.
